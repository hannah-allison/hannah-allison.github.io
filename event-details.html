--- layout: page title: Schedule and Details permalink: /schedule sidebar_link: True --- All of the evening's events will take place in the outdoor courtyard of Chabot Space and Science Center, a science center and museum located in the Oakland hills. The ceremony will begin at 6:30 pm. Afterwards, please join us for a cocktail hour and reception. Cocktail attire is requested.

Chabot hosts public telescope viewings every Saturday evening until 10:30 pm. After dinner, you're welcome to visit the upstairs Observatory Deck to (hopefully!) see views of the Bay and stars.
